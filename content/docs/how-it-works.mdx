---
title: How It Works
description: An overview of the core services powering the Aquanode platform.
---

Aquanode is built on three internal services that work together to provide a seamless GPU cloud experience.

---

## Mjolnir — Unified GPU API

Mjolnir is the central API layer that sits between Aquanode and all GPU providers. It exposes a single, unified API regardless of which provider is powering your deployment.

- Supports **web2 datacenters**, **web3 networks**, and **community-based providers**
- Abstracts away provider-specific APIs, pricing models, and access rules
- Makes it straightforward to add new providers without changing how the rest of the platform works

---

## Themis — Reconciliation Service

Themis ensures consistency between Aquanode's internal state and what Mjolnir reports from providers.

- Continuously reconciles your account balance and resource usage across providers
- Catches and corrects any drift between the Aquanode database and live provider data
- Keeps billing accurate and deployments in sync

---

## Ogre — Worker Service

Ogre is the background worker responsible for the operational health of your deployments.

- Monitors **GPU health** and **resource access** across active VMs
- Collects and reports **metrics** for your deployments in real time
- Powers platform features like **backups** (automated and manual snapshots) and **migrations** (moving workloads across GPUs)
