Migrations in Aquanode let you move a running workload from one GPU or provider to another. Your environment and data are preserved through the process using Aquanode's built-in storage.

---

## How It Works

1. **Take a snapshot** of your current VM (see [Backups](/docs/cloud-features/workspace-backup)).
2. **Launch a new VM** on your target provider or GPU type.
3. **Restore the snapshot** on the new VM.

Your environment will be available on the new GPU exactly as you left it.

## Select a Snapshot to Restore

On the new VM, use the **Restore Deployment** panel to choose the source deployment you want to migrate from. The dropdown shows available deployments along with their IDs and saved paths, so you can select the correct backup source.

![restore-deployment](/snapshots/new_restore_backup.png)

### Restored VM

Once the restore completes, the new VM comes up with the same saved state as the original deployment. This is the final step in migrating your workload to a different GPU or provider.
