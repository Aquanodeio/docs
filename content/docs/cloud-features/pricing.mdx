## Pricing Models

- **Voltage Park** — On-demand with long-term reservation discounts for large-scale workloads
- **DataCrunch** — Fixed base rate + dynamic pricing on storage configured
- **Hot Aisle** — On-demand AMD Fixed GPU pricing
- **Akash, Vast.ai** — Decentralized auction marketplaces; prices fluctuate with supply and demand, typically the lowest cost options
- **Hyperstack, Massed Compute, Cudo Compute** — On-demand with competitive fixed rates
- **Vultr** — On-demand, virtualized infrastructure
