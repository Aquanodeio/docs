<Step>
## Create your account

Start by creating your Aquanode account:

1. [Sign up here](https://console.aquanode.io/login)
2. Verify your email address
3. Complete account setup

</Step>
<Step>

## Deploy your first GPU instance

Now you're ready to launch your first GPU-powered instance:

1. Open the [Marketplace](https://console.aquanode.io/marketplace) page
2. Choose your GPU type and provider:

   - **RTX 4090** - Cost-effective for inference workloads
   - **A100** - Balanced performance for training and inference
   - **H100** - High-performance for large models
   - and many more

3. Select **Pre-Configured VM** template
4. Enter your required resources ( RAM, VCPU, and Storage ) and SSH public key.
5. Click **Deploy** to launch your instance

Your instance will be ready in 1-2 minutes.

</Step>
<Step>

## Connect to your instance

Once your deployment is running:

1. Click your deployment from the list
2. In the deployment details, find the **Connect** section
3. Copy the SSH command and paste in termial to access the instance

</Step>
<Step>

## Monitor your deployment

Track your deployment performance:

1. Visit the [Deployments](https://console.aquanode.io/deployments)
2. Click on > Icon to open details of a deployment
3. Monitor key metrics:

   - **Active Deployments**
   - **GPU Usage**
   - **Monthly Usage**

</Step>
<Step>

## Clean up

To avoid unnecessary charges:

1. Go to your [Deployments](https://console.aquanode.io/deployments)
2. Click on your running deployment
3. Click **Stop** to stop the instance

</Step>
</Steps>
